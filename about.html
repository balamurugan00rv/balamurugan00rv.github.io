As we know that, day by day traffic is increased rapidly which leads to fail the scheduled timetable of the transportation. Most of the people travel by the bus as it is a convenient and cheapest source of transportation. People who mainly travel by the bus are the students and the employee etc.Everyday we see people going late to work,students late to their classes, just because of they decide to wait for the bus instead of just using an alternate transportation. So, if buses get delayed is mainly result in the wastage of time of the student. In this paper, we proposed the concept about to track the bus to avoid the time wastage and to reduce the human efforts also to reduce the chance of missing the bus. The proposed system deals with overcoming the problems stated above. This website used to information about all the buses travelling in madurai local bus. This information overcomes the problems.
